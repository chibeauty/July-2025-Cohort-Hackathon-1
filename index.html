About Me

Welcome to my digital portfolio.

I’m Gloria Ngwu, a software engineering student with a passion for crafting engaging, accessible, and responsive web interfaces.

I love exploring how things work—sometimes by intentionally breaking them just to figure out how to build them better. My primary tools are JavaScript, React, and Tailwind CSS, which I use daily to create sleek and performant user experiences.

I’m currently deepening my front-end expertise while beginning to explore the backend to better understand full-stack workflows. I’m especially interested in creating web applications that not only look good but also feel intuitive and functional across all devices.

When I’m not coding, you’ll probably find me sketching out product ideas, digging through GitHub projects, or explaining to my friends why “it works on my machine” is (sometimes) a valid excuse. I enjoy building things that are fun, functional, and just a little unpredictable in the best way possible.
